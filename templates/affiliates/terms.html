Baysoko Affiliate Program Terms

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Affiliates must have an active Baysoko account, comply with platform policies, and provide accurate profile information.

Enrollment is completed when your affiliate profile is created and approved. Baysoko may review accounts for quality and compliance before activating referral tracking.

Referrals are tracked via affiliate links and cookies (30 days). Clearing cookies or private browsing may break attribution.

The referral window is 30 days from the last qualifying click. If multiple affiliates refer the same user, the most recent qualifying referral receives credit.

Commissions are earned on paid orders placed by referred users. Rates can change based on your tier or campaign.

Payouts are processed after commissions are approved. You may need to supply payout details for verification. Payout timing can vary based on risk reviews and payment method availability.

Promotions must be honest, avoid misleading claims, and clearly disclose affiliate relationships when required by law. Do not impersonate Baysoko or use deceptive ads.

Use Baysoko logos and trademarks only with permission. Do not register domains or social handles that closely resemble Baysoko branding.

Fraudulent referrals, self-referrals, misleading promotions, or incentivized traffic are not allowed and may lead to cancellation.

Commissions may be reversed if an order is canceled, refunded, disputed, or determined to be fraudulent.

Baysoko may suspend or terminate affiliate access for violations, abnormal traffic patterns, or policy breaches. Outstanding commissions may be held pending review.

Affiliate data is provided for performance tracking only. Do not resell, share, or misuse referral data outside the Baysoko platform.

For questions, contact support from your account or email the marketplace team.
